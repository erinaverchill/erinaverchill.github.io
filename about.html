An exercise in self-reflection

Erin Averill has traversed a serpentine career phylogeny––branching from existential crises in San Francisco’s tech sphere, to botanizing in New Mexico’s high deserts, to now indulging in delusional literary ambitions. She was a radio host for KNCE in Taos, and her words have been published online and in print. Raised in Southern California, she recently relocated to Santa Fe from Taos.
